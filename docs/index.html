Currently Learning

A list of resources that I am learning from in my free time at the moment

Programming Massively Parallel Processors, Fourth Edition

by Wen-mei W. Hwu, David B. Kirk and Izzat El Hajj

A book focused on parallel computing and GPU programming using Nvidia's CUDA. The authors delve into topics such as parallel algorithms, the CUDA programming model, memory architecture, and performance optimization strategies.

Computer, Enhance!

by Casey Muratori

A series of long-format videos and exercices focused on "Performance Aware Programming" where Casey introduces topics like CPU Architercture, Assembly Language, OS Fundamentals, Optimization Techniques, etc. With the focus on writting more optimized CPU-friendly programs that can take advantage of the capabilities of the modern hardware available.

Operating Systems - Three Easy Pieces

by Andrea Arpaci-Dusseau and Remzi Arpaci-Dusseau

A must-read book on the fundamentals of operating systems focusing on three fundamental areas: vistualization, concurrency and persistance.
